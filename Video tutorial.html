How to Embed a Video into a Website

Step 1: Find Your Video

Go to YouTube and choose a video you'd like to embed.

Step 2: Click the Share Button

Click on the "Share" button below the video title.

Step 3: Choose "Embed"

Click the "Embed" option from the popup.

Step 4: Copy the Embed Code

Click the “Copy” button to copy the HTML iframe code.

Step 5: Paste the Embed Code

Paste the embed code into your HTML file where you'd like the video to appear.
